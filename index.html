Day 1: Arrival & Dry Rides

Friday
09:30 AM ─ Drive from Coimbatore

Start early for a smooth 1-hour drive (~40 km) to Mettupalayam foothills.

10:45 AM ─ Resort Check-In

Check in at Avana Resort. Settle down, change, and unpack baggage.

11:15 AM ─ Black Thunder Amusement Zone

Focus on dry amusement rides, kids play areas, and family attractions.

  • Younger Kids (4yo): Carousel, Caterpillar, Toy Train.
  • Older Kids (11yo): Dragon Coaster, Dashing Cars.
  • Wildlife: Feed birds at Kookyland & visit the Aquarium.
05:30 PM ─ Resort Rejuvenation

Head to the resort, rest, and let the kids play indoor board games.

08:00 PM ─ Dinner at Thunder Café

A relaxed dinner at the resort. Try the multi-cuisine or order room service.

2

Day 2: Scenic Coonoor Tour

Saturday
08:00 AM ─ Lazy Resort Breakfast

Enjoy a heavy complimentary buffet breakfast. Prepare kids with sweaters.

08:45 AM ─ Shared Tempo Traveller Climb

A scenic drive uphill via 14 hairpin bends. Pit-stop at Burliar for fresh forest fruits like mangosteen.

10:15 AM ─ Sim's Park Botanical Walk

Exotic trees, safe walkways, and fun pedal-boating for kids in the pond.

12:00 PM ─ Tea Factory & Chocolate Tasting

Visit Highfield Tea Factory. See tea-making and taste fresh homemade chocolates.

01:15 PM ─ Lunch in Bedford Circle

Dine at Cafe Dieda for kid-friendly pizzas and Italian pastas.

02:30 PM ─ Dolphin's Nose & Catherine Falls

Breath-taking views of misty valleys and distant waterfalls.

05:15 PM ─ Drive back Downhill

Travel back down the mountain. Reach the resort for a warm shower and dinner.

Note: Must book tickets exactly 60 days in advance at 08:00 AM on IRCTC!
06:15 AM ─ Early Wakeup call

Collect pre-packed light breakfast boxes from the resort kitchen.

06:45 AM ─ Head to Mettupalayam Station

A 5-minute drive to the station to catch the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

07:10 AM ─ Board the Toy Train (NMR)

A spectacular, slow-moving historical steam engine ride. Over 3.5 hours of dramatic cliffs and deep forests.

10:30 AM ─ Reach Coonoor Station

Your pre-booked private 17-Seater Tempo Traveller will meet you at the platform.

11:00 AM ─ Continue Coonoor Sightseeing

Proceed to Sim's Park, Tea Factory, and viewpoints just like the car route.

05:15 PM ─ Return by road

The train only goes uphill once. The 17-Seater drives the families comfortably back downhill.

3

Day 3: Splash Day & Return

Sunday
08:30 AM ─ Heavy Buffet Breakfast

Eat well at the resort restaurant to fuel up for a heavy water day.

10:00 AM ─ Check-Out & Store Baggage

Check out of rooms. Safe-keep bags in the resort cloakroom or vehicle.

10:15 AM ─ Black Thunder Water Splash!

Wear synthetic clothes. Splash all day long in the massive water parks.

  • Younger Kids: Safe Kiddies Pool, Mushroom showers.
  • Older Kids: Giant Wave Pool, Volcano slide, Twister.
  • Whole Family: Thunder Rain (Dance) & Lazy River float.
04:00 PM ─ Dry Off & Collect Bags

Use change rooms to dry off and dress. Retrieve luggage from the resort.

04:30 PM ─ Return drive to Coimbatore

A short, 1-hour relaxed drive back home. Unpack, unwind, and rest!

Kid Safety & Comfort

  • Prevent Motion Sickness

    Ghat roads can cause dizziness in children. Keep windows slightly down for fresh air, drive slowly around the 14 bends, and avoid heavy oily foods before the climb.

  • Swimwear Regulations

    Black Thunder strictly mandates synthetic or nylon clothing for water rides. Pack them in advance for the whole family to avoid paying high rental rates at the venue.

  • Weather Swings

    While Mettupalayam foothills are warm, Coonoor peaks get quite chilly and misty. Dress the children in layers (t-shirt + sweater) that can be easily removed.
