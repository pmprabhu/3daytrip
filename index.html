🎯 Nearby Activities & Visits

Wildlife, dams, waterfalls & culture — all within easy reach

🐘

Top Slip — Anamalai Tiger Reserve

~35 km · 1 hr
Jeep safaris, elephant camp & guided treks. Spot elephants, gaur, deer, primates & hornbills. Safari: Morning 6:30–9:30 AM · Afternoon 3:00–5:30 PM Elephant Camp: Bathing 8–9 AM · Feeding 4–5 PM Fees: Entry ~₹50/person · Jeep safari ~₹150–300/person ⚠️ Book safari 1–2 weeks ahead — sells out on weekends!
📍 View on Google Maps
🐅

Parambikulam Tiger Reserve

~40 km · 1.5 hr
UNESCO biodiversity hotspot (accessed via Top Slip). Bus safari with naturalist + bamboo rafting on the reservoir. Bus Safari: ~3 hrs · First 8 AM, last 2 PM · ~₹150–500/person Bamboo Rafting: ~₹100–300/adult Fees: ~₹85/person + Topslip pass-through fees ⚠️ Best Sept–Jan (may close Feb due to wildfire risk)
📍 View on Google Maps
🚣

Aliyar Dam

~22 km · 40 min
Boating & kayaking, park, garden, children's play area & aquarium — a relaxed family favourite. Timings: Park 8 AM–6 PM · Boating 10–1 & 2–5 Fees: Entry ₹10–30 · Boating ₹40–150/person
📍 View on Google Maps
💦

Monkey Falls

Near Aliyar
Refreshing roadside cascade on the Valparai road — a fun splash & picnic stop. Best time: Post-monsoon (full flow)
📍 View on Google Maps
🛕

Masani Amman Temple

~16 km · 30 min
Famous temple with a unique 15-ft reclining idol of Goddess Masani Amman — a great cultural stop.

📌 Good to Know

  • Book Top Slip & Parambikulam safari permits 1–2 weeks ahead (esp. weekends)
  • Best season: October–January (lush greenery, full waterfalls)
  • Forest check-posts close ~4 PM — plan early starts
  • Pack snacks & water — few shops inside the reserves
